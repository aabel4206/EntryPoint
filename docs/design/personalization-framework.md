# Personalization Framework

## Purpose

EntryPoint provides transportation onboarding recommendations based on student background.

## International Students

Transportation onboarding includes:

- Bobcat Shuttle
- Campus Maps
- Ride Sharing
- Transportation Safety
- Texas Driver Licensing

## Domestic Students

Transportation onboarding includes:

- Parking Services
- Campus Maps
- Transportation Services

## Research Goal

Evaluate whether personalized transportation onboarding provides more relevant guidance than a generic onboarding process.
