# Novelty Summary

## Core Idea

EntryPoint focuses on personalized transportation onboarding for international students.

## Novelty

Traditional onboarding systems provide generic transportation information to all students.

EntryPoint adapts transportation resources and onboarding tasks to the needs of international students who may be unfamiliar with transportation systems in the United States.

## Cultural Component

International students may not understand:

- Campus shuttle systems
- Public transportation
- Ride-sharing services
- Parking systems
- Driver licensing requirements

EntryPoint provides onboarding resources tailored to these transition needs.

## Adaptability

Texas State is used as a pilot implementation. The framework can later be adapted to other universities by replacing transportation resources and onboarding templates.

## Monitoring Component

The system monitors transportation-related university webpages and detects changes to important onboarding information.
