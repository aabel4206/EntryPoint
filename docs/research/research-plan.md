# Research Plan

## Research Title

AI-Assisted Monitoring of University Resource Pages for New Student Support

## Research Motivation

New students frequently rely on university webpages for important onboarding information. However, webpage content can change throughout the year, causing students to miss updates or rely on outdated information.

This project investigates whether AI-assisted webpage monitoring can improve the accuracy and timeliness of information presented to students.

## Research Question

Can a personalized transportation onboarding framework improve transportation resource accessibility and onboarding effectiveness for international students compared to a generic onboarding approach?

## Objectives

- Develop a system that monitors selected Texas State webpages.
- Detect meaningful webpage changes.
- Generate summaries of detected changes.
- Evaluate the usefulness of the generated information.

## Methodology

The project will monitor selected Texas State webpages and periodically compare webpage content over time.

When changes are detected, the system will:

1. Identify changed content.
2. Store change records.
3. Generate summaries of detected changes.
4. Present the information through EntryPoint.

## Evaluation

The evaluation will focus on:

- Accuracy of change detection.
- Quality of generated summaries.
- Practical usefulness of detected updates.

## Expected Outcome

The expected outcome is a system that helps maintain current student resource information while reducing the effort required to manually monitor university webpages.

## Future Work

Future research may include:

- Support for multiple universities.
- More advanced AI summarization.
- Automated importance classification.
- Student notification systems.

## Research Contribution

The contribution of this project is the design and evaluation of a personalized transportation onboarding framework that adapts onboarding tasks and resources to the transition needs of international students.
