# Week 3 Progress Report

## Project Update

This week focused on refining the project scope based on feedback and transitioning EntryPoint into a personalized onboarding framework with transportation as the pilot implementation.

## Work Completed

* Narrowed project scope from a general onboarding platform to a transportation-focused onboarding pilot.
* Defined transportation resource categories:

  * Campus Shuttle
  * Parking Services
  * Public Transportation
  * Ride Sharing
  * Driver Licensing
  * Campus Maps
  * Transportation Safety
* Added transportation resources through the API.
* Verified database persistence and retrieval functionality.
* Updated project documentation to reflect the transportation pilot.
* Designed the initial personalization framework for transportation onboarding.
* Created transportation onboarding templates for international and domestic students.
* Refined the research question and project objectives.

## Research Direction

The project is now positioned as a personalized onboarding framework rather than a transportation application. Transportation serves as the pilot domain used to evaluate the framework.

The personalization component focuses on adapting onboarding guidance for international students who may be unfamiliar with transportation systems in the United States.

## Current Status

The database contains transportation categories and resources, and the project direction has been refined to align with research goals and scalability requirements.

## Next Steps

* Implement transportation checklist generation.
* Develop webpage monitoring functionality.
* Create scalability plans for future onboarding domains such as accommodation.
* Continue expanding personalization features.
